GEOGRAPHY

THEORY

UNIT 1: Origin of the earth, Interior of the earth; Theory of Isostasy: Earth movement: Folds and faults (their types only), Rocks and their classification

UNIT II: Earthquake and Volcanoes, Major landforms: Mountains, plains, Plateaus and Lakes

UNIT III: Weathering and Denudation: Normal cycle of erosion, Work of rivers, glaciers, ground water and winds

UNIT IV: Atmosphere: Composition and Structure; Insolation and Temperature; Atmospheric Pressure and Winds, Humidity and Precipitation, Cyclones

UNIT V: Hydrosphere: Temperature of ocean water: Salinity of Ocean; Ocean Deposits, Configuration, Tides and currents

2. Topographical Map-Interpretation of a Mountain area with the help of Cross and Longitudinal Profiles.

3. Slope Analysis-Wentworth's method.
